Privacy Policy - Commute Timer

Commute Timer respects your privacy. This app is designed to work entirely offline and does not collect or share personal data.

Information Collection

Commute Timer does not collect any personal information.

  • Does not require login or account creation
  • Does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or identifiers
  • Does not track your location
  • Does not use analytics or tracking tools

Local Data Storage

All commute data, including commute duration and optional notes, is stored locally on your device only.

  • Data never leaves your device
  • Data is not uploaded to any server
  • Data is not synced or backed up by the app

You are fully in control of your data.

Data Sharing

Commute Timer does not share any data with third parties.

  • No data is transmitted externally
  • No data is sold, rented, or disclosed

Third-Party Services

Commute Timer does not use

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising services
  • Location services
  • Tracking SDKs

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app from your device. Uninstalling the app permanently removes all stored commute data.

Children’s Privacy

Commute Timer does not knowingly collect data from children. Because the app does not collect personal information, no child-specific data is stored.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the revised version will be made available within the app and on the app’s official listing page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer through the email address provided on the app’s store listing.
